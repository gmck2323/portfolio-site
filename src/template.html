About me

My career began a few years ago, when I was offered an entry level job working with basic HTML templates. I had no idea the level of passion I would develop for engineering. What started off as simple HTML/CSS turned into more complicated projects and promotions as I attempted to quench my thirst for more and more knowledge. If there's one thing I know, it's that I still have a lot to learn, and I can't wait to keep on pushing myself to see how much I can contribute.

In all things in life, I try to learn as much as I can while keeping an open heart (big cheese alert) and open mind. My ultimate goal in life is to make a difference wherever I can, for people, for companies I work for, and for the causes I believe in.
